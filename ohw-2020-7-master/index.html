About Us

FONAH Foundation is a Foundation setup on October 1st 2019. The foundation was setup to undertake a number of communal services that will foster National Development. We have executed some projects over the years while we still have some on the pipeline. The Foundation was setup by youths which majorly consists of young gradtuates and undergraduates that are concerned about contributing their quota to National Development.
